Hortonworks Inc (NASDAQ:HDP) stock is rallying more than 14%
in the opening hour of the trading session following its better than expected
second quarter results, yesterday. Hortonworks managed to report loss per 
share of $0.80, beating analysts' estimated loss per share of $0.83. 
Moreover, revenues clocked in at $30.6 million that came ahead of its
revenue estimates $23.3 million. Analysts at Pacific Crest reiterated its 
Overweight rating on the stock along with upward revision in its price target 
from $32 to $33, highlighting its increasing strategic value.
